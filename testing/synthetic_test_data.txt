Test 1
Type: Professional / Urgent

Other Person: Can you send me the updated report before 8 PM today?
Me: Yes, I am working on it.
Other Person: Please don’t delay, it’s urgent.

Expected Short Reply:
Sure, I’ll send it before 8 PM.

Expected Friendly Reply:
Sure, I’ll finish it and send it before 8 PM.

Expected Professional Reply:
Understood. I will send the updated report before 8 PM.

What to check:
- Understand urgency
- Professional tone
- Reply to latest message
